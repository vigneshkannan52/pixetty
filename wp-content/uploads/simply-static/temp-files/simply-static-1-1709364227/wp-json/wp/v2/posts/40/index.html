1. Use Classic Backgrounds to Highlight the Subject<\/h4>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

More often than not, fine art portraits have very simple backgrounds that put the spotlight on the subjects. Having a very simple background will highlight every feature on your subject\u2019s face, like skin textures, clothes, and the objects they\u2019re holding.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

Many fine art portraits have black backgrounds. This creates the illusion of depth and compels the eye to focus exclusively on the model.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n
